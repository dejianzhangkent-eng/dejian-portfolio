## Portfolio Case Study Template

Use this structure when turning a PDF into a portfolio page:

1. Project title
2. One-sentence summary
3. Role and responsibilities
4. Team size and timeline
5. Tools used
6. Problem / challenge
7. Research or analysis
8. Design / strategy process
9. Final outcome
10. Reflection and what you would improve

## Quick Selection Guide

- For UX or product design roles: prioritize `05`, `03`, and `02`.
- For marketing or business roles: prioritize `07`, `06`, and `09`.
- For research or technology policy roles: prioritize `08` and `04`.
- For general applications: use resume plus the strongest 3-4 projects instead of showing every file.
